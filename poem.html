To My Baggo ✨

This isn’t a birthday poem. It’s just me admitting that life got way more interesting the day you started annoying me. You tell me about your day like a story you wrote, the highs, the lows, every little note. I listen, smiling, lost in your tone, making your moments feel like my own. Somewhere between your stories and laughs, I started hoping for more than just chats. If the future ever asks what I’d choose, I think the answer would look a lot like you. You tease me when I get soft with my words, play it cool like you haven’t heard. But then you’re the one turning shy, smiling, looking away, passing me by. That flip from teasing to quiet grace yeah, that’s the moment I can’t replace. You’re small in size but act so tough, put on a front like you’re made of stuff. But I know the truth you try to hide a soft little heart on the inside. You act nonchalant, cool in your way, like nothing ever makes you stay. But when you get chalant, sweet and true, that’s when I fall a little more for you. Soooooooo Happy Birthday Baggo!
